About Us

Kasitech (AUS) Pty Ltd is a specialised IT consulting firm established in 2022, focused on delivering high-quality performance engineering, non-functional testing, and automation solutions.

With over two decades of industry experience, Kasitech brings deep expertise across complex enterprise systems spanning Government, Payments, Telecommunications, and Insurance domains. We partner with organisations to ensure their applications are scalable, resilient, and capable of handling real-world demand.

Our approach goes beyond traditional testing. We focus on end-to-end performance engineering — combining advanced tooling, architectural insight, and data-driven analysis to identify bottlenecks, optimise system behaviour, and improve overall user experience.

By aligning performance strategy with business goals, we help organisations minimise risk, improve system stability, and deliver high-performing digital platforms.

  • ✔ Expertise in performance testing, engineering, and automation
  • ✔ Strong background in cloud (AWS), microservices, and APIs
  • ✔ Proven experience across Government, Telecom, Payments, and Insurance sectors
  • ✔ Hands-on with tools like LoadRunner, JMeter, Gatling, Dynatrace and Splunk

Kasitech has strong expertise in modern technology landscapes including cloud platforms, microservices, and distributed systems. We actively work with leading performance and observability tools such as LoadRunner, JMeter, Gatling, Dynatrace, Splunk, and AWS Cloud services.
